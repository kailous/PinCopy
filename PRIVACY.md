Privacy Policy for PinCopy
1. Information We Collect

Website Content: PinCopy accesses image elements on pinterest.com to provide the collection feature.

User Activity: We store metadata (image URLs, page titles, and source links) of images you explicitly choose to "collect".

2. How We Use Information

Local Storage: All collected data is stored locally within your browser using the chrome.storage.local API.

Functionality: Data is used strictly to display your collection in the sidebar and to facilitate features like downloading or creating moodboards.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to any third parties.

No Remote Tracking: No data is sent to external servers or remote databases; everything stays on your local machine.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Users have full control over their data. You can delete individual items or clear the entire collection at any time through the sidebar interface.
